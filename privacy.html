Privacy Policy

Like millions of other website owners, we use Google Analytics on bloomsol.com

Google Analytics is a piece of software that grabs data about visitors. It’s something like an advanced server log.

What does Google Analytics record?

  • * What website you came from to get here?
  • * How long you stay for?
  • * What kind of computer you’re using?
  • * Demographics and Interest Reports
  • * And quite a bit more.

What do we do with your data?

The tracking information allows us to better understand the kind of people who come to our site and what content they’re reading. This allows us to make better decisions about design and writing. All of our activity falls within the bounds of the Google Analytics Terms of Service.

Want to opt out of tracking?

You can also opt out of Google’s advertising tracking cookie or use a browser plugin to opt out of all Google Analytics tracking software.
